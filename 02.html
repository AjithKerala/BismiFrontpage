BISMI HYPERMARKET DEPARTMENTS

Bismi group, the emerging giant in white goods business in Kerala is taking a step closer in serving the
customers with Hyper Market division – Bismi Hypermart Private Ltd.

Hypermarkets which will have combination of both food & Electronics spread out in two floors. We will be
also having Supermarkets as our second format which will have complete food assortment. All Major A & B
towns will have Hypermarket & its corresponding C towns (Feeder Market) will have supermarkets.

We as a group has a vision to bring differentiation and innovations in retail sector that will add values to all
customers & stakeholders. In line with the above, we have already planned certain unique selling
propositions in our outlets which we would like to keep it confidential at this point of time. We will share
those as and when time permits.
We will put our best efforts to make all the execution at shop floor a flawless one.
